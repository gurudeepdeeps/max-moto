Window Tint Packages

Why Choose Window Tinting?

  • ✓ Heat Rejection – Keeps interiors cooler and reduces AC load.
  • ✓ UV Protection – Blocks up to 99% of harmful UV rays.
  • ✓ Interior Protection – Prevents fading and cracking of seats and dashboard.
  • ✓ Enhanced Privacy – Tinted windows keep prying eyes away.
  • ✓ Stylish Look – Available in multiple shades and finishes.
  • ✓ Improved Driving Comfort – Reduces glare for safer driving.

Types of Window Tints We Offer

  • ✓ Dyed Film – Affordable, basic tint with style enhancement.
  • ✓ Carbon Film – Long-lasting tint with better heat rejection.
  • ✓ Ceramic Film – Premium, high-performance tint with superior UV and IR rejection.
  • ✓ Clear UV Film – Transparent film that blocks UV without changing visibility.
  • ✓ Legal-Compliant Tints – Films that comply with Indian RTO regulations.
