The Max Moto Story

Founded on the principle that every vehicle deserves a showroom-quality finish, Max Moto Detailing began as a boutique studio specializing in high-end paint correction. Today, we are proud to be one of Bangalore's most trusted names in Ceramic Coating and Paint Protection Film (PPF).

Our team consists of certified master detailers who treat every project as a work of art. Using only the world's finest chemicals and state-of-the-art technology, we ensure your investment is protected against the harsh elements of Indian roads while radiating a glossy, deep-mirror finish.

Our Mission

"To provide car enthusiasts with a service that transcends typical detailing. We aim to deliver unmatched durability, aesthetic brilliance, and long-term protection for the most prized automotive assets."
