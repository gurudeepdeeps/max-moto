Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you visit Max Moto Detailing, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and vehicle details when you voluntarily provide them via our contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our detailing services, process your appointments, and communicate with you about your vehicle's maintenance. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, to request corrections, or to request the deletion of your data from our records.
