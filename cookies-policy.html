Cookies Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience. They allow the website to remember your preferences and improve overall performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

Max Moto Detailing uses cookies for basic analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site and to ensure a smooth scrolling experience with our animation libraries.

3. Third-party Cookies

We may also use trust-worthy third-party analytical tools that place cookies to track anonymous usage statistics and improve site speed.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies, though this may impact certain site functionalities.
